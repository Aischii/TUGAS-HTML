PRODUCT DETAILS

Discover unparalleled gaming performance with the X2H v3 wireless gaming mouse. Engineered with a distinctive high hump and narrower waist, it is designed for claw and palm-claw grips, ensuring optimal comfort and precision during intense gaming sessions. Equipped with the advanced XS-1 sensor boasting 32000 DPI sensitivity, 750 IPS tracking, and 50g acceleration, the X2H v3 delivers flawless accuracy and rapid responsiveness. Weighing a mere 53g, this ultra-lightweight mouse enhances agility without compromising on durability. With customizable settings, an impressive 8K polling rate, and seamless wireless connectivity, elevate your gaming experience with the X2H v3's unmatched combination of speed and control.
